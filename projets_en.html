Capteur de vitesse infrarouge Two infrared sensors placed at a known distance, which allows us to calculate the speed of an object that moves in front of it.
Example : we place the sensors at 10cm from each other, ig both sensors doesn't detect anything, we wait. when the first on detects something in font of it
we start a chronometer that we stop when the second one detects the object too. then we can do time/10 which gives us the time the object needs to travel 1cm,
then we can just switch to kilometers and hours to get the object's speed in km/h.
